Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

We believe in simple, honest data practices.

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Scroll ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide you with a great experience:

  • Usage data — Anonymous analytics to understand how features are used and to improve the app.
  • Crash reports — Diagnostic data sent when the app crashes, to help us fix bugs quickly.
  • Support correspondence — Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting our support team.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect or store your personal content.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data storage & security

All data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized team members. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

Third-party services

Scroll may use trusted third-party services (such as Apple's frameworks) that have their own privacy policies. We only work with services that uphold strong privacy standards.

Children's privacy

Scroll is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
